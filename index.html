The Sheltowee Trace is the longest trail in the Daniel Boone National Forest and connects many state parks and a national park trail systems. My map design choices try mimic the colors, symbols, and forms found in the area mapped. The theme of the map will often have a distinct style to distinguish those features from reference features; developing the figure-ground relationship is essential for good interpretation.
